KKN Desa Penari

The KKN film in Penari Village contains the story of six students named Nur (Tissa Biani), Widya (Adinda Thomas), Ayu (Aghniny Haque), Bima (Achmad Megantara), Anton (Calvin Jeremy), and Wahyu (Fajar Nugraha) who are doing KKN in Penari village, a village that has very strong mystical powers.
