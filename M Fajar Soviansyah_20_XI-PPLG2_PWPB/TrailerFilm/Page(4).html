Di Ambang Kematian

On the Verge of Death tells the story of Nadia's family consisting of Mr. Suyatno (Teuku Rifnu Wikana), Mother (Kinaryosih), Yoga (Wafda Saifan), and Nadia (Taskya Namya). The story begins when Nadia and Yoga find their father entering a room with a sack. The sack fell and it was discovered that it contained a goat's head. After catching their father, not long after their mother died horribly in 2002. After their mother's death, Nadia and Yoga often experienced incidents of terror from scary spirits.
